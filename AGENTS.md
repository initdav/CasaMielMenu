# Casa Miel design guidance

Preserve the original light, creamy menu treatment in future visual work.

## Brand palette

- Olive: `#7B8149`
- Ochre: `#BF8412`
- Honey: `#F2DC99`
- Cream: `#E1D5BF`
- Brown: `#392010`

## Original color roles

- Main menu/page background: warm paper `#F7F1E5`.
- Primary text, headings, outlines, and active category controls: brown `#392010`.
- Search field and elevated light surfaces: warm white `#FFFAF0`.
- Supporting light text: cream `#E1D5BF`.
- Hero overlay and navigation: translucent brown `#392010` over the ochre mosaic artwork.
- Hero accents and circular calls to action: ochre `#BF8412` and honey `#F2DC99`.
- Footer background and restrained interactive accents: olive `#7B8149`.

Do not replace the creamy main background with brown. Maintain readable contrast while using this original role assignment unless the user explicitly requests a new color treatment.
